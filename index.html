Our Story

A Journey Through
Georgian Flavors

Nestled in the heart of Kutaisi, Satori was born from a deep love for Georgian culinary traditions. Our name, meaning "sudden enlightenment," reflects our belief that great food can transform an ordinary moment into something extraordinary.

Every dish we serve tells a story—of ancient recipes passed down through generations, of fresh ingredients sourced from local farmers, and of our unwavering commitment to authentic flavors.
